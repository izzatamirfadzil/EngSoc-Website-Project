World Robotic Center Competition 2025

(WRCC 2025)

With the inspiring theme, “Empowering the Future: Robotics for a Smarter World,” the competition attracted participants from Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand and Singapore. These budding innovators demonstrated their ingenuity and technical prowess in seven categories: Sumo Robots, Soccer, Maze Runners, Wi-Fi Car Adventures, Line Tracer, Transporter and Creative Robotics.
